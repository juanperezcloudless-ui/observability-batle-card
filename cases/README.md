# Growth Use Cases

This document outlines three simulated growth use cases: Start, Growth, and Enterprise. Each scenario describes the cost drivers for Instana, Dynatrace, and New Relic based on publicly referenced pricing units.

## Use Case Scenarios

1. **Start**: 
   - Suitable for small businesses beginning their observability journey.
   - Cost drivers include basic monitoring features and support for a limited number of users.

2. **Growth**: 
   - Designed for medium-sized companies expanding their infrastructure.
   - Higher costs driven by increased nodes, users, and advanced monitoring features.

3. **Enterprise**: 
   - Tailored for large organizations with complex architectures.
   - Significant cost impacts from extensive usage across multiple environments, advanced analytics, and support options.

## Cost Drivers
- **Instana**: Pricing based on the number of hosts monitored and additional features like APM and infrastructure monitoring. More info: https://www.instana.com/pricing/
- **Dynatrace**: Offers various pricing models based on the entity types (hosts, applications) and advanced features. More info: https://www.dynatrace.com/pricing/
- **New Relic**: Charges based on data ingest, user seats, and additional features. More info: https://newrelic.com/pricing

---

*All prices are subject to change and should be verified on the respective vendor's site for the most accurate and current information.*
